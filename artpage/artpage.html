General Information

Here, you will find my completed art projects including (but are not limited to) comics, collages, full-color works, and sketches. Unfortunately I lost some of my older works on my other PC so they will not be featured here (but if it makes you feel better, they were atrocious because I was mainly messing around for most of them). I will post whatever projects I can get my hands which includes works that I may or may not have featured previously.

Why post art projects here? Will you stop posting art on your usual hotspots?

I am posting art here because I feel it will be easier to catalogue and manage my projects through this channel as opposed to using previous means of establishment. I am not going to stop posting on my other profiles though, although most of the content will be late releases of what I post on here and more traditional-oriented, such as sketches, concept art, and character designs.

How Often Will This Page be Updated?

As long as I have the time and motivation to create art. Sometimes life gets in the way of what you enjoy and that's okay. As long as the world exists, life will be a block of liberty that has to be managed. But I'm divagating a bit here. All you need to know is that I will attempt to keep creating art for as long as possible.

Well, that's all done and dusted. More information might be added to this bloc in the future so still tuned. Enjoy the art!

-T.S.
