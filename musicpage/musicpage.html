Why upload here? Why not do it on YouTube/Facebook/Twitter...(any other platform you can name...)?

Very simple. One word: Copyright. Most of the sites you can think of have very strict copyright rules, so much so that even if a song that may be copyrighted is played for only a second, the entire audio is sacrificed, ultimately disrupting the overall flow of the track. I don't think you'd want to hear audio with static-y portions and neither do I.

Also, as a side reason, I want to personalize the way I upload files/projects. I do not want to rely on other domains to host my files (even though this project will probably be hosted on a domain-hosting service such as GitHub). Surely I can't be fully independent from third party services, but I am looking to minimize it as much as possible.

In a certain sense, this personal website is a project in it of itself. I only recently started learning HTML and CSS (started approx. June 28, 2020 or so), so I think this is a good barometer of my capabilities.

What Type of Music Can I Expect?

Well, I am not musically gifted to make my own music, so I primarily use others' works and mix and match them together. I create mashups, medleys, and mixes (wow, much m's such wow ). I'm also hoping to get into remixing tracks, but at the moment, it seems far away.

How Often Will This Page be Updated?

As long as I have the time and motivation to work on projects, I will keep this page updated. As I mentioned in the homepage, I might be late with some updates because of college/life/other factors you're possibly theorizing about, so please have some patience.
With that being said...enjoy the projects!
